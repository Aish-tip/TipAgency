Our clients wanted to convert their ancestral property into a weekend getaway targeted at city-dwellers and tourists alike. While the homestay perfectly suited the needs of travelers hungry for authentic Indian trips, we used the natural charms of Kandavara to entice our audience.

View Project

Exploring through
sight and sound

With the website, we wanted to recreate the idyllic ambience of this heritage home set in Kundapur. We employed audio-visual and literary devices to give an overall experience of what their stay in the coastal mansion would look and feel like. We used various devices to recreate this vibe: from shooting a stop-motion video of the banana leaf meal they served up to maintaining an active Instagram handle which whips up quite a stir with out picturesque updates from this heavenly location.
