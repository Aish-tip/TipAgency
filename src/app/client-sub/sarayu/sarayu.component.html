Sarayu is a development with flats ranging from 6,300 sq ft. to 10,300 sq ft. Sarayu offers occupants the freedom of flowing spaces. The walls are not meant to be constraints but merely a canvas that residents can utilise to make this space their own.

View Project

Intelligent design forms the backbone of this principle, with flowing spaces seamlessly merging to provide residents a welcome reprise from conformity and clutter. Created with a distinctive design that offers residents malleability of their space, in their homes the focal point is the residents and their experience.
