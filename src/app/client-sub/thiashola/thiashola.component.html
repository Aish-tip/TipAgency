Making minimal comprehensive

Being avid tea-lovers ourselves, one of our most creatively rewarding undertakings was for this exclusive Nilgiri tea estate brand. We wanted to create a website for this fine locally-grown tea that would speak to tea connoisseurs across the globe and back home, alike.

View Project

A thousands words to a picture

Minimal yet comprehensive was our oxymoronic brief while creating a website that was easy to navigate while being rich in information. Trying to put across this information, succinctly, but not scrimping on the details was a challenge our tech and design teams were happy to sink their teeth into.

In sync with their logo and packaging, we created an elegant website design that would make it stand apart from the other local brands on a supermarket shelf. Finding just the right colour palette that relayed the calm freshness of Thiashola tea; we reviewed several options together with our client before settling for the perfect fit.
